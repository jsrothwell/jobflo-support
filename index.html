Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a job?

On iPhone, tap the + button. On Mac, click Add Job in the toolbar. You can also share job postings directly from Safari using the Share Sheet — tap Share, then select Jobflo.

What is Vibe Check?

Vibe Check lets you rate your gut feeling 🔥 😐 💀 at each stage of your job search — per job and per interview round. Over time, Jobflo tracks how well your instincts predict your outcomes in the Insights view.

What are Multiplier Skills?

For each job, you can tag a skill that makes you stand out specifically for that role or team. Jobflo tracks these across your search so you can spot patterns in what's landing.

Is my data private?

Yes. All your data is stored locally on your device. Jobflo does not collect or transmit your personal information.

Is Jobflo available on both iPhone and Mac?

Yes! Jobflo is a universal app available on iPhone and Mac.
